About Us

Kunafa, is a premium Middle Eastern dessert made of shredded dough, layered with Cheese or Cream filling, garnished with Pistachios, gently cooked on fire or baked, it is consumed warm after pouring an Aromatic Saffron syrup on it. (Richness of Pistachios, Delicious toppings, amazing fillings with Sweet Saffron syrup) - A True Delight to the Taste Buds!

It’s known that Kunafa is a 1000 years old recipe, now incorporated with international variety of flavours by Kunafa House Team to bring to you. All the way from the Middle East to India with refreshing combinations and flavours!

Origin Of Kunafa House:

Founders: Sumayya Samreen & Mohamed Kazim started Kunafa House in 2021, with the first outlet in Nugambakkam, Chennai.

IDEA

The Love of Kunafa started in the childhood days of Kazim, who lived in Dubai and his parents frequently took him to have his favourite Arabian Dessert. When his wife Sumayya had a Kunafa for the first time, she Loved it and it became her favourite dessert.

 When she came to India, she always craved for a Kunafa, and that's when she came up with the Idea She thought that like her there would be many who would be craving to have a Delicious Kunafa or would Love it once they try it out in India. That was the moment - The Birth of the Idea!

The Husband-Wife duo have bought the Exotic Kunafa to India in its Authentic Form, as well as created some amazing innovative flavours never seen before!

It was on July 2021, the first outlet was started in Nungambakkam, Chennai. Having had 5 outlets by 2024. And the Rest is History.

The Love received from customers kept The Kunafa House Team motivated to keep going and made a Vision of making "Kunafa House" the "Most Loved Dessert Outlet in India".

Facts About Kunafas in Kunafa House

Kunafa House has designed their Kunafa in a unique a way, that every customer has control over the level of sweetness in their Kunafa - The unique creation of Kunafa House!

Kunafa made at Kunafa House comes with an Aromatic Saffron Syrup separately, this Syrup needs to be poured on the Kunafa to make it sweet, Hence, you decide how the Sweetness!

An Ideal Dessert for Mild-Sweet Lovers! - The only dessert ever to have the option of choosing its degree of Sweetness!  Making it suitable for all.
